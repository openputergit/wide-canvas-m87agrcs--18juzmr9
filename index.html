Robotics & AI Glossary

Key terms and concepts to help you navigate the world of robotics and artificial intelligence

Machine Learning

A subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming. Common types include supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.

Neural Networks

Computing systems inspired by biological neural networks designed to recognize patterns. Deep neural networks with multiple layers can learn complex representations of data.

Computer Vision

A field of AI that enables computers to derive meaningful information from digital images or videos, allowing machines to identify and classify objects.

Natural Language Processing

The ability of computer systems to understand, interpret, and generate human language, facilitating interaction between humans and machines through speech or text.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Technology that automates repetitive tasks by configuring software robots to capture and interpret applications for processing transactions, manipulating data, and communicating with other systems.

Model Ensemble

A technique that combines multiple learning algorithms to improve overall prediction performance, often resulting in better accuracy and robustness than individual models.
